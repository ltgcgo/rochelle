Rochelle demos

Learn how to use Rochelle. Watch it in action. Evaluate if it fits your needs.

Text Reader

An experiment and showcase on textRead. A stream is consumed, and text is read line-by-line.

DSV Parser

An experiment and showcase on dsvParse. In goes in a stream from a DSV, and out emits a table.

Tree Converter

An experiment and showcase on treeConvert. From anything to anything that can be represented as a path notated with integers, the possibilities are limitless.
